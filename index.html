Who We Are

At Paws in Heaven, your local pet cremation service provider, we understand how difficult it is to lose a pet. We use our compassion and expertise in memorializing pets and final disposition to make your bereavement a bit easier.

Services

Paws in Heaven offers private (individual) and communal cremation options. In private cremation—where your pet’s ashes are returned to you in an engravable urn—we place your pet in a private cradle with an identifying metal tag. We also offer communal cremation, where your pet is cremated in a shared receptacle and their ashes are spread on our farmland alongside those of other pets. With communal cremation, you will receive an ink print of your pet’s paw to commemorate their life and time spent with you.

No matter the services you choose, your pet will be treated with the same gentle care we would give our own.

Transport Services

We can accept pre-arranged drop-offs at our office or can arrange at-home collections and drop-offs during business hours. Emergency after-hour pick-ups can be arranged. Please call the office to set up any pick-ups or drop-offs. Costs vary by location and pet size. Standard home pick-up within Ponca City limits during business hours is free. Emergency home pick-up within Ponca City limits outside of business hours is a minimum fee of $95. All pick-ups coordinated outside of Ponca City limits may incur a mileage fee.

What is Cremation by Aquamation?

Aquamation is a gentle, eco-friendly, water-based cremation of your pet’s remains. It uses water flow, warm temperatures, and an alkaline solution to mirror the natural process when a pet is laid to rest in the soil.

Why Choose Aquamation?

  • Gentle and respectful process
  • Zero emissions - no harmful greenhouse gases
  • Low energy consumption
  • 1/10 the carbon footprint than flame-based cremation
  • Ashes are 100% clean, disease and pollutant free
  • Cost is similar to standard flame-based cremation

Why Choose Paws in Heaven?

Packages and Additional Options

BACK TO NATURE

COMPANION

Following private cremation, receive your pet's ashes in an urn (laser engraving optional) along with a clay paw print, fur clipping, certificate of cremation, and your pet's stainless steel cremation identification tag.

Following private cremation, receive your pet's ashes in a premium wood urn (laser engraving optional) along with a clay paw print, fur clipping, an ink nose print, a custom resin memento with fur clippings, certificate of cremation, and your pet's stainless steel cremation identification tag.

ADDITIONAL OPTIONS

Private or communal cremation packages can be combined with the following options: a premium wood or metal urn, laser engraving, ink paw and nose prints, clay paw prints, fur clippings, and resin-based mementos with fur clippings.
